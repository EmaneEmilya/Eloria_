## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach:** Drawing inspiration from premium beauty e-commerce sites (Glossier, Rare Beauty, Sephora) combined with Balea's accessible, natural aesthetic. Soft, feminine, and product-focused design that emphasizes affordability and gentle care.

## Typography System

**Primary Font:** Poppins (Google Fonts)
- Hero Headline: 600 weight, 3xl-6xl responsive
- Section Headings: 600 weight, 2xl-4xl
- Subheadings: 500 weight, lg-xl
- Body Text: 400 weight, base-lg
- Buttons/CTAs: 500 weight, sm-base

**Accent Font:** Playfair Display (Google Fonts)
- Brand tagline/slogan: 400-600 weight, italic for emphasis
- Section decorative titles: 600 weight

## Layout & Spacing

**Spacing Scale:** Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32
- Section padding: py-20 (desktop), py-12 (mobile)
- Container max-width: max-w-7xl
- Grid gaps: gap-8 (desktop), gap-6 (mobile)
- Component spacing: space-y-4 to space-y-8

**Grid System:**
- Product Categories: 2x2 grid (lg), 2 columns (md), 1 column (mobile)
- Featured Products: 3 columns (lg), 2 columns (md), 1 column (mobile)

## Visual Effects

**Animations:**
- Fade-in on scroll for sections (gentle, 0.6s duration)
- Subtle hover transforms on cards (scale 1.02-1.05)
- Button hover: slight brightness increase
- Smooth transitions (transition-all duration-300)

**Shadows & Depth:**
- Cards: soft pastel-tinted shadows
- Buttons: subtle shadow with blur
- Elevated elements: layered shadows for depth

**Shapes & Borders:**
- Rounded corners throughout (rounded-lg to rounded-2xl)
- Smooth gradients for backgrounds
- Organic shapes as decorative elements

---

## Images

**Hero Section:**
- Large background image: Soft-focus beauty/skincare scene with pastel tones (flowers, cream textures, natural light)
- Dimensions: Full viewport width, 70-80vh height
- Overlay: Pastel gradient (pink/lavender) at 40-60% opacity

**Product Category Images:**
- 4 lifestyle images representing each category
- Square format, soft lighting
- Natural, minimal aesthetic

**Featured Product Images:**
- Actual Balea product photography (white background or lifestyle shots)
- Consistent aspect ratio (3:4 or 1:1)
- High quality, clean presentation

**About Section:**
- Decorative background: Soft botanical illustration or abstract organic shapes in pastel tones
- Subtle, non-distracting

**Overall Image Treatment:**
- Soft edges (slight border radius)
- Pastel color grading
- Natural, feminine aesthetic
- Professional product photography style
